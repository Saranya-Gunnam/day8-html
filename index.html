ABOUT US

Welcome to Nail Salon, where beauty meets relaxation. Established in 2024, our salon has been a trusted name in providing exceptional nail care and spa services. We are passionate about enhancing your natural beauty and creating a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.At Nail Salon, we believe in offering more than just services – we deliver an experience. From our friendly, skilled technicians to our clean and welcoming environment, everything is designed to ensure your comfort and satisfaction.

SERVICES:

Our Services At Nail Salon, we offer a wide range of professional nail care and beauty services tailored to your preferences. Whether you're looking for a classic manicure, intricate nail art, or a relaxing spa pedicure, we’ve got you covered.

Manicures:

Transform your hands with our range of manicure:

  • Classic Manicure: A simple and elegant treatment for clean, polished nails.
  • Gel Manicure: Long-lasting shine with chip-resistant gel polish.
  • French Manicure: Timeless, chic design for any occasion.
  • Deluxe Manicure: Includes exfoliation, moisturizing masks, and a relaxing hand massage.

Pedicures:

Pamper your feet with our soothing pedicure treatments:

  • Classic Pedicure: Perfectly groomed toenails and softened skin.
  • Spa Pedicure: Includes exfoliation, callus removal, and a hydrating foot massage.
  • Gel Pedicure: Beautifully polished toes with lasting gel color.
  • Luxury Pedicure: Indulge in paraffin treatments, cooling masks, and therapeutic massages.

Nail Extensions & Enhancements:

Get the length and shape you desire:

  • Acrylic Nails: Durable, stylish, and customizable extensions.
  • Gel Extensions: Lightweight and natural-looking options.
  • Dip Powder Nails: Strong, long-lasting, and odor-free enhancements.
